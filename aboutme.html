Hello, I am Jessica Donley, an artist passionate about video games!

I'm currently an undergraduate junior at SUNY University at Albany, with a Computer Science Major and an Art Minor. I've been drawing and coding since I was young.

Digital art is my main art medium, which I have been using since I was 14 years old. I have created my own characters and stories, and I love to create fan art of my favorite characters.

Gaming is something that I've had an avid interest in since I was little. I grew up playing video games, and as such have a huge fondness for. I love to incorporate it in my work, whether that be art, coding, or working on my own video game projects. I personally play games using PC, Xbox, and have played on Nintendo and Sony consoles in the past. My favorite genre of games include RPGs, action, beat em' ups, and roguelikes.

I've had an interest in coding since I was in elementary school, starting with Scratch when I was about 10. Once I reached high school, I joined the school's robotics team. I quickly became the lead programmer, eventually becoming the captain for my junior and senior years. After graduating, I became a mentor for the team for 2 years. Through robotics, I learned Java, and thus decided to pursue a degree in computer science.


My main mediums in art consist of digital, charcoal, graphite, and gouache. I love to work with illustration, such as concept art, large scale drawings, and comics.

My main programming languages are Java, C, HTML, and CSS. I use GameMaker Studio 2 for game development. When pushing my code to Github, I use GitBash.
